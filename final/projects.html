Coffee Analysis
Brief
 I worked with a database of different coffee beans to analyze how different growing factors impacted flavor aspects
Code
 I used pandas to read in the csv file and then used varying machine learning modules to predict how certain variables would change flavor ratings and also analyze how closely related growing variables are to the flavor ratings.
Presentation
 I presented a poster with my hypotheses, information, and findings to the class. Many of the growing variables were strongly correlated with the flavor rating, although altitude and location were not.
